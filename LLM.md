NLP - Sunfield of AI that focuses on developing technologies that are capable og understanding , interpreting and generating human language 

Language Model - Model that learns to predict the next word in a sequence based on what came before
Types of LM - 
1. Stats Era - N grams , Markov - Text prediction while typing 
2. Neural Era - RNN , LSTM - Google's Word2Vec
3. Transformers - Encoder - Decoder , Decoder only - LLMs

Large Language Model - Tranformer based Language Model thats trained at massive scale . While the core idea is still "predict the next token but the scale and architecture makes the difference .
Types of LLMs - 
Encoder-Decoder - T5
Encoder only - Bert
Decoder only - GPT

Tokenization - Breaking down text into smaller units called tokens ( words , subwords ,characters , or even setences depending on the level of granularity)

Embeddings - Vector representations of words that attempt to capture its meanings.
Types of Embeddings
Static - Same for all the processing
Contextual - Changes as per the sentence and context
